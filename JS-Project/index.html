AMY PHILLIPS

Photo & Video Portfolio

Amy Phillips is a freelance photographer and videographer. Her work focuses on topics of gender and sexuality through culture and movement. Their artistic lens captures the nuances of queerness, enticing viewers to question their own preconceptions and see through new eyes.

A recent graduate from the University of Colorado Boulder in Media Production with minors in Political Science, Journalism, and Creative Technology and Design, Phillips now resides in Los Angeles and hopes to connect with other creatives and make some beautiful work.
